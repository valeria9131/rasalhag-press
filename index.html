The Artist

Meet Aria Vance

Aria Vance is a fine line ink illustrator based in Seattle, Washington. With a background in classical printmaking and botanical draftsmanship, Aria’s work explores the therapeutic relationships between line architecture, spatial density, and meditative patterns.

Her transition into publishing adult coloring books was born from a desire to democratize high-end illustration. Rather than keeping her original hand-drawn plates locked behind gallery collections, she invites colorists worldwide to become co-creators of her art.

"A line is a path of concentration. By filling the white spaces I create, my hope is that you find your own slow, quiet rhythm and escape the digital noise of the screen."
